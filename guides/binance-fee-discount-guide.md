# Binance Fee Discount Guide

Crypto exchange fees can include spot trading fees, futures trading fees, withdrawal fees, funding payments, conversion spreads, and network fees. Beginners often focus only on the advertised trading fee, but the total cost can be broader.

## Common Ways Users Reduce Fees

- Using a valid referral code during registration.
- Holding or using the exchange token for eligible fee discounts.
- Increasing trading volume to qualify for a higher VIP tier.
- Choosing lower-cost networks for withdrawals when safe and supported.
- Avoiding unnecessary conversions between assets.

## Referral Fee Discount Notes

A referral fee discount may be shown during signup or inside the user account. The exact percentage, product coverage, and duration can change. Always check the current terms displayed by Binance before relying on a discount.

## Beginner Warning

Lower fees do not make a trade profitable. Trading risk, leverage risk, liquidity, funding rates, and market volatility matter more than small fee differences.
