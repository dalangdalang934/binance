# Binance Registration Guide for Beginners

Binance registration is the process of creating an exchange account, verifying identity when required, and securing the account before using spot or futures products.

For a practical walkthrough, see the [HoldWins Binance registration guide](https://holdwins.com/).

## Basic Registration Flow

1. Open the official Binance website or verified app download page.
2. Create an account with an email address or phone number.
3. Enter a referral code if you plan to use one.
4. Complete identity verification if required in your region.
5. Enable two-factor authentication before depositing funds.
6. Review fee, withdrawal, and product availability for your location.

## Safety Checklist

- Check the domain carefully before entering credentials.
- Avoid search ads that imitate exchange brands.
- Do not install apps from unknown APK download sites.
- Use a unique password and a dedicated email address.
- Enable anti-phishing codes if the exchange supports them.

## Notes About Referral Codes

Referral codes may offer fee discounts or other benefits, but details can change by region, account type, and exchange policy. Always verify the displayed fee information during signup and inside the account dashboard.
