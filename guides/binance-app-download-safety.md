# Binance App Download Safety Checklist

Fake exchange apps are a common risk for new crypto users. A fake app can steal login credentials, recovery phrases, two-factor codes, or deposit addresses.

## Safer Download Practices

- Use the official exchange website as the starting point.
- Verify the app publisher in the iOS App Store or Google Play.
- Avoid APK files from unknown Telegram groups, forums, or ad pages.
- Check that the domain spelling is correct before downloading.
- Be careful with sponsored search results that imitate official brands.

## Before Logging In

- Confirm the app name and publisher.
- Compare the app link with the official website.
- Update your operating system and browser.
- Use two-factor authentication.
- Set an anti-phishing code if available.
