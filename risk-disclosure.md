# Risk Disclosure

Digital asset trading is risky. Prices can move quickly, liquidity can change, platforms can restrict products by region, and leveraged products can cause rapid losses.

This repository is for education only. It is not financial advice, investment advice, legal advice, or tax advice.

Before using any crypto exchange:

- Confirm whether the service is available in your jurisdiction.
- Understand all fees and withdrawal rules.
- Avoid leverage if you do not fully understand liquidation.
- Do not trade with funds you cannot afford to lose.
- Verify all website and app links before entering credentials.

No guide, referral code, or fee discount can remove trading risk.
