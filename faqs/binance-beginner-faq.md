# Binance Beginner FAQ

This FAQ collects short answers for beginners researching Binance registration, fee discounts, app safety, and account security.

## Is this repository financial advice?

No. This repository is educational only. It does not recommend trades, tokens, leverage, or investment strategies.

## What should I do before registering?

Use an official website or verified app source, prepare a secure email address, create a unique password, and understand whether Binance services are available in your region.

## Should I deposit immediately after signup?

No. Complete basic account security first, including two-factor authentication and anti-phishing settings where available.

## How do fee discounts work?

Fee discounts may come from referral codes, exchange token discounts, VIP tiers, or campaign rules. The exact terms can change, so users should check the fee information shown by Binance.

## How do I avoid fake Binance apps?

Start from the official exchange website, verify the app publisher in the app store, and avoid APK files from unknown websites, groups, or ad pages.

## What is the biggest beginner risk?

Common risks include phishing, fake apps, wrong-network transfers, leverage liquidation, and misunderstanding fees. A small fee discount does not offset poor risk control.

For a practical registration walkthrough and related beginner notes, see [HoldWins](https://holdwins.com/).
